About Us

Narsingdi Government Girls High School is a well-established educational institution located in Shatirpara, Narsingdi. The school has a rich history of nurturing young girls through quality education, preparing them for their future endeavors. It emphasizes not just academic excellence but also personal development, making it a special place for learning.

Vision & Mission

The school's vision is to empower girls through education, ensuring they become confident and responsible citizens. Its mission includes fostering a learning environment that encourages critical thinking and ethical values.

Achievements

The school has received various accolades for academic performance and participation in cultural and sports events, reflecting its commitment to excellence in education.

Admission Process

Admission Requirements

New students must meet age criteria and submit necessary documents, including birth certificates and previous educational records.

Application Process

Prospective students should fill out the application forms, attach required documents, and submit them to the school administration.

Important Dates

Admission deadlines and open days will be announced each year, typically at the beginning of the academic session.
